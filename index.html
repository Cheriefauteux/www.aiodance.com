Our Story

All In One Dance Co, established in 2005, has been a consistent presence in the performing arts landscape ever since. With our instructors boasting decades of experience in teaching, our Artistic Director, a graduate of Simone Fraser University's dance program, leads the way. Additionally, our ballet instructor holds a certification from the Royal Academy of Dance (RAD). Embracing diversity, we offer a spectrum of dance styles. Our ethos, aligned with our brand's philosophy of ERA (Express, Reach, Aspire), centers on empowering each dancer to articulate their unique expression, strive for the stars, and pursue their individual aspirations.
