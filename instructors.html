Instructors

Artistic Director

Suzanne Balamatowski is the esteemed Artistic Director of All in One Dance Co., boasting a rich history in the world of dance. Her illustrious career commenced over two decades ago, marked by a tenure teaching dance since 1996 across various esteemed studios. In 2005, Suzanne realized her vision by founding her own studio. Holding a distinguished Bachelor of Arts degree in Dance from Simon Fraser University, her profound expertise, vast skill set, and boundless creativity are the pillars of excellence that define our studio's greatness.

Cherie Fauteux

Cherie holds the esteemed role of Jazz, Hip-hop, Zumba, Preschool, and Lyrical instructor at All in One Dance Co. Her journey in dance education and choreography began in 1999 across various reputable studios before she became an integral part of AIO Dance Co in 2005. Cherie's performances have graced esteemed stages including those at West Edmonton Mall, Disneyland, Disneyland Parades, and Las Vegas dance conventions. Notably, she was part of the Cheer and Dance team that clinched provincial championship in 2000. Over the years, her students have garnered numerous accolades and cash prizes at prestigious dance competitions. Cherie's wealth of experience and unwavering passion continually enrich the offerings at All in One Dance Co.

Aimee Kelm

Aimee stands as our esteemed certified RAD ballet instructor, bringing with her extensive classical ballet training and professional expertise in ballroom and breakdance. Her versatility extends to teaching Breakdance, Contemporary, Ballroom, and Musical Theatre classes at our studio. With a career spanning back to 2002, Aimee has been dedicated to teaching dance and choreography across various studios before becoming an integral part of AIO Dance Co in 2005. Under her guidance, her students have achieved numerous awards and cash prizes. Aimee's performances have graced prestigious stages including those at West Edmonton Mall, Disneyland, Disneyland Parades, and Las Vegas dance conventions. Her profound knowledge and boundless creativity continue to elevate AIO Dance Co to new heights.
